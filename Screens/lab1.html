Instructions

This lab was an introductory lab to OpenGL. In this, lab I needed to:

  • Draw 10 shapes
  • Use at least 3 colors
  • Use at least 1 GL_POLYGON
  • Use at least 1 GL_QUAD_STRIP
  • Use at least 1 GL_TRIANGLE_STRIP
  • Use at least 1 GL_TRIANGLE_FAN
  • Use at least 1 GL_LINE variant

My Implementation

  • I decided to draw a two dimensional house using the OpenGL primatives listed above.
  • There are a total of twelve shapes in the picture.
  • The house was created in two dimensional space using simple shapes.
  • The sky is a combination of a few triangle strips and fans.

GL_POLYGONS:
  • House Base
  • All Three Windows
  • Door
  • Dirt
  • Grass
GL_QUAD_STRIP:
  • Roof
GL_TRIANGLE_STRIP:
  • Sky triangles to the left and right of the roof
GL_TRIANGLE_FAN:
  • Sky squares to the left and right of the house base
GL_LINE:
  • Door Knob (Line Loop)
